A word about this experience

This is not a traditional course.

Chaos· Alignment· Belonging· Crisis· Transformation

Inspired by the symbolic architecture of Antoni Gaudí, this experience invites you to explore Scrum through five evolving spaces.

Like a cathedral under construction, teams grow through structure, focus, adaptation, and collaboration.

As you follow Alex and his team, you move through spaces of chaos, alignment, crisis, reflection, and transformation. Each room represents a stage of agile teamwork. Each symbol reflects a deeper idea.

Observe how scattered tasks slowly become a shared vision — how uncertainty turns into clarity — and how a disconnected group becomes a focused, agile team.

Enter the spaces. Explore the architecture. Build your first Sprint.

CHAPTER 4 — INTRODUCING THE SPRINT
La Catedral del Sprint · Alex names what is happening

One Sprint. 4 weeks. One arch.

Alex turns to the team. He picks up the single card they have just prioritized — the rest stay on the wall.

ALEXFor the next four weeks we work on only this one thing. Nothing else from the backlog. Just this goal — then we look at what we built, and start again.
SPRINTA short, time-boxed work cycle — typically two to four weeks. The team commits to one Sprint Goal and protects that focus until the cycle ends.
